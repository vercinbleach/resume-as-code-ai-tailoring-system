## Actionable findings

### 1. Resume length and depth

- Detected length: 328 words.
- Assessment: slightly too short for the detected career level.
- Recommendation: add approximately 80 words, with at least four additional
  bullet points. The platform also warns against adding more than roughly 370
  additional words.

Our assessment: adding four evidence-backed bullets is feasible because the
current one-page layout still has unused vertical space. The additions should
come from the existing knowledge base and should not be filler.

Strong candidates for the next revision:

- Ansible proof of concept for zero-touch provisioning of Cisco, Fortinet, and
  Aruba firewalls.
- ProcessMaker workflows connecting CRM, ERP, and internal systems through
  REST and SOAP APIs.
- Three-month colleague training and operational handover at Innovery.
- A quantified Power BI result, once the actual number of dashboards, KPIs,
  departments, users, or hours saved is confirmed.

### 2. Repeated action verb

Resume Worded found one repetition issue: `Built` is used three times.

Affected bullets:

1. Java/Python microservices at Grup Montaner.
2. The integration ecosystem at Innovery.
3. The AI Document Support Assistant.

Recommended revision:

- Change the Montaner bullet to start with `Developed`.
- Change the Innovery integration bullet to start with `Engineered` or
  `Integrated`.
- Keep `Built` for the AI Document Support Assistant.

This fixes the repetition without using decorative synonyms that weaken the
meaning.

### 3. Quantified impact

The report says the resume already quantifies achievements well and treats
further quantification as optional. It specifically selected this line as an
example without a measurable result:

> Delivered executive Power BI dashboards with centralized strategic KPIs for
> department heads and leadership.

Before rewriting it, confirm at least one real measurement:

- Number of dashboards delivered.
- Number of KPIs centralized.
- Number of departments or regular users served.
- Reporting time reduced or hours saved.
- Reporting frequency or decision cycle improved.

No number should be added until it is supported by the knowledge base or
confirmed directly.

### 4. Dates and ordering

Resume Worded flags inconsistent dates or ordering, but the explanation is
locked behind Pro. The experience order itself is reverse chronological and
appears correct.

Likely formatting trigger, based on our own inspection rather than confirmed
platform feedback: the resume mixes full month names with `Sept`, while other
systems commonly expect either full names everywhere or three-letter
abbreviations everywhere.

Suggested normalization:

- `Jul 2025 - Present`
- `Sep 2024 - Jul 2025`
- `May 2023 - Sep 2024`

Keep `Expected graduation: 2027` as explicitly requested.

### 5. Leadership and soft skills

Leadership, teamwork, drive, and initiative are surfaced as improvement areas,
but their detailed checks are locked behind Pro. The current resume already
contains `Architected and led` and a three-person collaboration, so the problem
may be insufficient depth rather than a complete absence of evidence.

The most defensible addition is the existing three-month mentoring and handover
experience. If available, also confirm the number of collaborators,
stakeholders, departments, or users involved in the Grup Montaner platform.

## Locked or incomplete checks

- Possible spelling errors.

These should not be represented as confirmed defects without additional
evidence.
