# Ecommerce Final Project

## Context

This was an iFP academic final project and one of Vincenzo's first experiences
collaborating with other developers on a shared codebase. The team built a
complete ecommerce prototype in Symfony.

## What the application does

The application provided separate customer and administration areas around a
dynamic product catalog.

Customer functionality included browsing the complete catalog, adding products
to a shopping cart, and completing a simulated payment flow. The payment was a
demonstration and was not connected to a real payment provider.

Administration functionality allowed products to be created or edited,
including fields such as name, image, and price. The exact set of catalog
operations and permission rules is no longer recalled.

## Solution

- PHP and Symfony backend.
- Database-backed dynamic product catalog.
- Shopping-cart flow.
- Simulated checkout and payment flow.
- Separate customer and administration sections.
- Administration interface for modifying product names, images, prices, and
  other catalog fields.
- Collaborative development across a shared academic codebase.
